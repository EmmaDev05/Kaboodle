Kaboodle

Kaboodle first hit the stage in early 2017 as an independent ticketing operation but has over 15 years experience running ticketing and travel operations for numerous, award-winning, festivals and events.
Kaboodle is a unique ticketing solution that offers event organisers and consumers a combined ticketing, accommodation and travel management system all on one platform.
We currently work with a large number of festivals, exhibitions and live events to ensure that customers receive maximum choice and promoters maximum revenue opportunities.

Why Kaboodle?

We offer a dynamic and versatile solution to clients wanting a flexible event, group or ticket focused system, incorporating travel and accommodation.
We are not just a plug-in or add-on, we are your complete solution.
Kaboodle acts as the primary ticket agent for a variety of independent festivals, events and venues in the UK, Canada and across Europe.

  • Smooth, customisable and fully branded booking experience,specifically designed for upsell

  • Retain your fanbase and keep ownership of your data

  • Easy upsell post-purchase with the Kaboodle customer account

  • Sell flight and accommodation packages through our ATOL bonded tour operation

  • Simple to use resale platform.
    Maximise revenue and attendance, discouraging secondary markets

  • Experienced and dedicated account management and customer support from onsale to onsite
