PHP Engineer

We're looking for confident and ambitious PHP Engineers to join our growing team to support the ongoing development of our industry leading booking platform.
You will help drive our ambitious product roadmap, improve the performance and stability of our systems and develop software engineering best practices across the organisation.

You'll be responsible for:

  • Delivering innovative web functionality using OOP PHP.

  • Building new features with consideration for testability and extensibility.

  • Working on our in house booking and reservation system, building new features whilst helping to improve the overall architecture for our diverse client base.

  • Be involved in technical discussion and become a subject matter experts

  • Encouraging innovation and help support an environment to help build an award-winning software solution.

You and your skills

  • A background in commercial development, (within the travel or ticketing industries would be a bonus!)

  • A continuous learning mindset and understanding of software patterns

  • Solid understanding of OOP PHP and MySQL

  • jQuery , CSS, XML

  • Experience with Kohana or a similar PHP framework

  • Production experience with Linux / Unix environments

  • Travel Industry experience

  • A passion for wanting to develop the best and most ground-breaking software you can.

  • A love of the internet and travel.

  • Good attention to detail and testing.

  • Comfortable working as part of a team.

The Package

  • Competitive salary
  • Extraordinary work experience and team culture
  • 23 days holiday per annum (increasing with service to a maximum of 25 days)
  • Benefits including a Cycle-To-Work scheme, a Health Cash Plan and the opportunity to work at some of the best events and festivals on the planet!
